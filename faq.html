Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Buy Now," and follow the checkout process. You can pay via Bkash, Nagad, or Cash on Delivery.

Is there a warranty on used devices?

Yes! All used devices come with a 7-day replacement guarantee and a 1-year service warranty.

How long does delivery take?

Inside Dhaka: 24-48 hours. Outside Dhaka: 3-5 business days via courier.

Can I sell my old phone here?

Yes, click the "Sell" button in the menu, upload your device details, and we will give you a quote instantly.

What is your refund policy?

If the product has a defect not mentioned in the description, you can return it within 3 days for a full refund.
