Building Trust in Electronics

TechTown is Bangladesh's dedicated marketplace for verified new and used electronics. We prioritize transparency, safety, and informed buying decisions.

The Problem We Solve

The electronics resale market is full of confusion. Buyers struggle to know if a "Like New" phone is actually broken, and honest sellers struggle to build credibility without a proper platform.


Our Solution: TechTown bridges this gap. We offer a transparent marketplace where condition standards are strict, and every product whether new or used is clearly categorized. We help individuals like Robiul buy with confidence and sellers like Limon grow their business.

Who We Serve

Everyday Buyers

People who want reliable electronics at fair prices but fear being misled about a device's true condition.

Individual Sellers

Casual sellers dealing in used electronics who need a professional platform to build trust with buyers.

Business Vendors

Shops and repair businesses that need inventory management, branding, and analytics tools.
