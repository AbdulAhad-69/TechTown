Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At TechTown, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how TechTown ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. By using TechTown, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

A. Personal Information

When you register as a buyer or seller, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Shipping and billing addresses.
  • For Vendors: NID (National ID) copies, Trade License documents, and business details for verification purposes.

B. Transaction Information

We collect details related to your purchases, sales, and listing history on our marketplace.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following specific purposes:

  • To process orders and manage shipping.
  • To verify the identity of sellers (Individuals and Organizations) to prevent fraud.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, orders, or support requests.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Vendor Verification Data

Safety is our top priority. Documents submitted for vendor verification (such as NID or Trade License) are stored securely and are accessed only by authorized TechTown administrators for approval purposes. These documents are never shared publicly.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information with:

  • Logistics Partners: To deliver your products (e.g., sharing your address with the courier).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by Bangladeshi law or to protect against fraud and illegal activities.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information stored in your TechTown profile. If you wish to delete your account permanently, please contact our support team.
