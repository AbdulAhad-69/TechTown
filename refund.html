Refund & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is protected by our transparent return process.

Note: You must initiate a return request within 3 days of receiving your item.

At TechTown, we want you to shop with confidence. If you receive a product that is defective, damaged, or significantly different from the seller's description, you are eligible for a return and refund.

1. Eligibility for Returns

You may return an item under the following conditions:

  • Defective Product: The device does not turn on, has software issues, or hardware failure not mentioned in the listing.
  • Wrong Item: You received a different model, color, or storage capacity than what you ordered.
  • Not as Described: The condition (e.g., scratches, dents) is significantly worse than the photos or description provided by the seller.
  • Fake/Counterfeit: The product is proven to be non-genuine.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Returns will NOT be accepted if:

  • The return request is made after 3 days of delivery.
  • The item is damaged by the buyer (e.g., dropped, water damage).
  • The item has been tampered with or opened (warranty seals broken).
  • You simply changed your mind (unless the seller specifically agrees).

3. The Return Process

  1. Step 1: Contact Us
    Email support@techtown.com.bd with your Order ID and photos/video of the issue.
  2. Step 2: Ship the Item
    Once approved, pack the item securely with all original accessories and ship it to our Returns Center.
  3. Step 3: Inspection
    Our technical team will inspect the device within 48 hours of receipt.
  4. Step 4: Refund
    If the defect is confirmed, we will initiate your refund.

4. Refund Method & Timeframe

Refunds are processed to the original payment method (Bkash, Nagad, or Bank Card).

  • Mobile Banking (Bkash/Nagad): 3-5 business days.
  • Bank Cards: 7-10 business days (depending on your bank).

Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

5. Warranty

Some "New" items may come with an official brand warranty. In such cases, please contact the brand's authorized service center directly. For "Used" items, TechTown offers a 7-day checking warranty only.
